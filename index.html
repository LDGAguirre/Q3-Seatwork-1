Elemental Archons

Character Information

Furina

Furina, also known as Focalors, is the Goddess of Water and rules over the nation of Fontaine, which is a nation which is based on water.

Zhongli

Zhongli is the God of Earth and Contracts who also goes by the name "Rex Lapis." He also rules over the nation of Liyue which is the city of contracts and is very strict about contracts.

Nahida

Also Known as Lesser Lord Kusanali, Nahida is the God of Dendro which means she is bascally the Goddess of Plants, and she rules over the city of Sumeru, which has lots of flaura and fauna.

Raiden Shogun

Raiden Shogun is also known simply as Ei, and is known to be a ruthless Goddess and Ruler over the nation of Inazuma, but once you get to know her, she's a pretty good person. She is the Goddess of Thunder and Lightning and has her own realm which is called the Plane of Euthymia.
